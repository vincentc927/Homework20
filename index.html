Whiskers Re-Design

For Web & Mobile

A volunteer-run site dedicated to saving cats and
placing them in loving homes.

Research & Ideation

THE PROBLEM: Animal lovers find that pet adoption websites are difficult to navigate, forms take time to complete, and adoption fees often do not itemize details.

THE SOLUTION: Simplifying the Whiskers website to make it more user friendly by redesigning its information architecture and providing understandable outreach.
